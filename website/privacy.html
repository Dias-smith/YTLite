Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

YouLite (“we”, “the app”) is a hybrid YouTube / music-style client for iOS and Android. This policy describes what information the apps may process and why. We do not sell your personal data.

Information we process

  • Account data. If you sign in with Sign in with Apple or Google (via our backend), we may store identifiers needed for authentication and profile display (such as a user id, display name, and avatar URL when provided) through our cloud provider (Supabase). Apple may share a private relay email address with us if you choose Hide My Email.
  • Library & preferences. Playlists, likes, history, subscribed channels, language, and night-mode settings may be stored on device and, when you are signed in, synced to your account.
  • YouTube access. Optional YouTube read-only scopes (Google sign-in) may be used to show subscriptions and related YouTube data you authorize. We do not post or modify your YouTube content through these scopes. Apple-only accounts do not receive YouTube OAuth tokens unless you also link Google access where offered.
  • Analytics. We use Firebase Analytics to understand aggregated app usage (for example feature engagement and session metrics) so we can improve the product. This data is processed by Google Firebase under their terms and privacy policy.
  • Diagnostics. Crash and performance reports (Firebase Crashlytics) may be collected in release builds to improve stability. You can limit diagnostic sharing through your device OS settings where available.

How we use information

To provide playback, library sync, sign-in, product analytics, and to fix bugs. We do not use your library content for advertising profiles, and we do not sell personal information.

Third parties

The app relies on third-party services such as Apple (Sign in with Apple / App Store), Google (sign-in / YouTube APIs / Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics), and Supabase (backend). Their policies apply to data they process. Streamed media and metadata come from YouTube and remain subject to YouTube’s terms.

Data retention & deletion

Local data remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall. Account-linked library data can be removed by using in-app account deletion (where available) or by contacting us. Backups on our servers are purged according to our operational retention windows after a valid deletion request.

Children

YouLite is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
